Privacy & Data Governance Policy

Effective Date: February 3, 2026 | Imperfect Irissio Media

This Privacy Policy describes how Imperfect Irissio ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information across our digital platforms, content services, and internal software operations.

1. General Data Collection

We respect the privacy of our audience. When you interact with our content, website, or social media channels, we may collect:

  • Usage Data: Information on how you access our content (e.g., page views, clicks).
  • Communication Data: If you contact us directly via email or social platforms.

We use this information strictly to improve our content strategy and respond to inquiries.

2. Internal Software Operations (Imperfect Irissio CMS)

Transparency Statement: Imperfect Irissio utilizes proprietary internal software to manage our digital publishing workflow. This section explicitly governs the data usage of our internal application, the Imperfect Irissio CMS.

A. Application Purpose

The Imperfect Irissio CMS is a workflow automation tool designed strictly for internal use. It connects our internal content database to our Pinterest Business account to streamline the scheduling and publication of our original artwork and articles.

B. Pinterest API Data Usage

To function, this application interacts with the Pinterest API. We access the following data types:

  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth2 tokens used to maintain a secure, authorized connection.
  • Boards & Pins: Read and Write access to create content on our own boards.
  • Profile Data: Read-only access to confirm identity.

C. Data Protection & Limited Use

Our use of information received from Pinterest APIs adheres to the Pinterest Developer Platform Terms.

We do not: Sell, rent, or transfer API data to third parties.
We do not: Use API data for surveillance or lending purposes.
We do: Use the data solely to execute internal publishing tasks.

3. Third-Party Services

We may utilize trusted third-party services for hosting and analytics, including GitHub Pages and Google Cloud. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and data protection laws.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or our internal data practices, please contact us via our official Pinterest profile messaging system.
